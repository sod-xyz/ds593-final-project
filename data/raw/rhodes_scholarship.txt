Overview:
Rhodes Scholarship (United Kingdom). The Rhodes Scholarship is one of the oldest and most prestigious international scholarship programs, enabling outstanding students from around the world to study at the University of Oxford. Eligibility depends on specific country quotas, and applicants must demonstrate academic excellence, leadership, and commitment to service.

Degree Level:
- Master's / PhD

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers Oxford course fees, annual stipend, and travel support.
