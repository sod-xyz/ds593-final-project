NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program (United States citizens, nationals, or permanent residents)

The NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program supports outstanding graduate students in research-based master's and doctoral degrees in STEM fields. It is a major U.S. fellowship but is restricted by citizenship or permanent residency.

Mongolia Eligibility:
- Mongolian citizens are not eligible unless they are U.S. citizens, U.S. nationals, or U.S. permanent residents.

Eligibility:
- Applicants must be U.S. citizens, U.S. nationals, or permanent residents at the time of application.
- Applicants must pursue or plan to pursue a research-based master's or doctoral degree in an eligible STEM field.
- Applicants must meet NSF rules on prior graduate study and previous fellowship acceptance.

Degree Level:
- Research-based Master's / PhD

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency is required for U.S. graduate study, but the fellowship itself is primarily citizenship- and field-based.

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Provides annual stipend and cost-of-education allowance for eligible graduate study.
