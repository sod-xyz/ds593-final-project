Asian Development Bank–Japan Scholarship Program (Multiple countries)

Overview:
Asian Development Bank–Japan Scholarship Program (ADB-JSP). The ADB-Japan Scholarship Program is open to nationals of ADB borrowing member countries who have gained admission to an approved master's course at a designated institution. It supports postgraduate study in fields related to development, economics, management, science and technology, and other areas relevant to development in Asia and the Pacific.

Degree Level:
- Master's

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers full tuition, monthly subsistence and housing allowance, books, medical insurance, and travel.
