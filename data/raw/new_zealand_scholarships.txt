New Zealand Scholarships (New Zealand)

Overview:
New Zealand Scholarships (New Zealand). New Zealand government scholarships have supported students from eligible countries to study in New Zealand or the Pacific, often with a development focus. Eligibility depends heavily on country, priority sector, and scholarship type. Applicants should check the official New Zealand scholarships website for current eligible countries, study levels, and application cycles.

Degree Level:
- Undergraduate / Postgraduate depending on country

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers tuition, living allowance, establishment allowance, travel, medical insurance, and other study costs.
