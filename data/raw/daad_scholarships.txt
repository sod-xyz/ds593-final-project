DAAD Scholarships (Germany)

Overview:
DAAD Scholarships (Germany). DAAD provides scholarship programmes for international students, graduates, doctoral students, postdocs, and researchers who want to study or conduct research in Germany. DAAD scholarships vary by programme, field, degree level, and applicant country. Some programmes support master's study, some support doctoral research, and some focus on development-related postgraduate courses. Applicants should use the DAAD scholarship database to check eligible programmes, academic requirements, language requirements, funding duration, and application documents.

Degree Level:
- Master's / PhD / Research

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: varies
- Details: Depends on program (German or English requirements vary)

Funding:
- Funding type: varies
- Details: Funding depends on the specific DAAD programme; many include monthly stipend, insurance, travel allowance, and sometimes tuition-related support.
