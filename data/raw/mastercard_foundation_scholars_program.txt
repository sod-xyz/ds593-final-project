Overview:
Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program. The Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program partners with universities and organizations to support talented young people, especially from Africa and other eligible contexts depending on partner rules, to access education and leadership development. The programme often combines scholarship support with mentoring, leadership training, transition support, and community impact goals. Eligibility depends on the partner institution and programme.
