ADB Internship Program (Multiple countries)

Overview:
ADB Internship Program (Asian Development Bank). The ADB Internship Program provides opportunities for students pursuing postgraduate studies to gain experience through research assignments and professional work at the Asian Development Bank. It is not a full scholarship but offers exposure to development work and international organizations.
