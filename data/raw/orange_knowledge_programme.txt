Orange Knowledge Programme (Netherlands)

Overview:
Orange Knowledge Programme (Netherlands). The Orange Knowledge Programme was a Dutch scholarship programme focused on strengthening knowledge and capacity through education and training for professionals from selected countries. It supported short courses and master's programmes in the Netherlands for eligible professionals. Applicants should verify the current status of the programme and eligible country list because Dutch scholarship programmes and names can change over time.

Degree Level:
- Short course / Master's / Professional training

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: conditional
- Details: English proficiency typically required for courses

Funding:
- Funding type: partial
- Details: Usually provides contribution toward tuition, visa, travel, insurance, and living costs; exact coverage depends on course and institution.
