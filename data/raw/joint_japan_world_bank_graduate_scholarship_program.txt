Joint Japan/World Bank Graduate Scholarship Program (Multiple countries)

Overview:
Joint Japan/World Bank Graduate Scholarship Program (JJ/WBGSP). The Joint Japan/World Bank Graduate Scholarship Program supports citizens of eligible developing countries with relevant professional experience who are applying to approved master's programmes in development-related fields. The programme focuses on areas such as economic policy, tax policy, infrastructure management, and other development topics. Applicants must usually be admitted to a participating master's programme to be eligible.

Degree Level:
- Master's

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required for participating master's programs

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers tuition, monthly living stipend, travel, and health insurance for approved master's programmes.
