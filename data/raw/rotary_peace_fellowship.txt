Rotary Peace Fellowship (Multiple countries)

Overview:
Rotary Peace Fellowship. The Rotary Peace Fellowship supports leaders with work experience in peace and development. Rotary awards fellowships for master's degree programmes and professional development certificate programmes at Rotary Peace Centers. The master's programme usually lasts 15 to 24 months and includes applied field experience, while the certificate programme is intended for experienced working professionals.

Degree Level:
- Master's / Professional certificate

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers tuition and fees, room and board, transportation, and internship or field-study expenses.
