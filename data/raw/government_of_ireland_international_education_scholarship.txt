Government of Ireland International Education Scholarship (Ireland)

Overview:
Government of Ireland International Education Scholarship (Ireland). Ireland offers government and higher education scholarships for international students. The Government of Ireland International Education Scholarship is a competitive scholarship route for high-caliber international students studying in Ireland. Applicants should check Education in Ireland and the relevant Irish higher education institution for eligible programmes, award value, application process, and deadlines.

Degree Level:
- Bachelor's / Master's / PhD depending on programme

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required

Funding:
- Funding type: partial
- Details: Typically provides a stipend and full fee waiver for one year, depending on the specific award rules.
