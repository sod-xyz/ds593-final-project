Gates Cambridge Scholarship (United Kingdom)

Overview:
Gates Cambridge Scholarship (United Kingdom). The Gates Cambridge Scholarship supports outstanding applicants from outside the United Kingdom to pursue a full-time postgraduate degree at the University of Cambridge. The program selects scholars based on academic excellence, leadership potential, and commitment to improving the lives of others.

Degree Level:
- Master's / PhD

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers university composition fee, maintenance allowance, airfare, visa, and health surcharge.
