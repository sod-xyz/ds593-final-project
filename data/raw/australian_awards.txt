Australia Awards (Australia)

Overview:
Australia Awards (Australia). Australia Awards Scholarships provide citizens of Mongolia and other participating countries with opportunities to obtain postgraduate qualifications at Australian tertiary institutions. The program is a bilateral development scholarship and is designed to build skills and knowledge that recipients can use to contribute to development in their home country. Applicants should check the Mongolia-specific Australia Awards website for eligibility, priority fields, English requirements, work experience requirements, and return-home obligations.

Degree Level:
- Postgraduate / Master's

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required (IELTS/TOEFL typically required)

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers tuition, return travel, establishment allowance, living allowance, health cover, and academic support.
