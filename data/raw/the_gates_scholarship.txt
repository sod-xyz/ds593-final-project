The Gates Scholarship (United States high school seniors only)

The Gates Scholarship supports outstanding low-income high school seniors in the United States. It is different from Gates Cambridge and is not an international graduate scholarship.

Mongolia Eligibility:
- Mongolian citizens are not eligible unless they are U.S. citizens, nationals, or permanent residents and meet the U.S. high school and Pell eligibility requirements.

Eligibility:
- Applicants must be high school seniors.
- Applicants must be Pell-eligible.
- Applicants must be U.S. citizens, U.S. nationals, or permanent residents.
- Applicants must plan to enroll full-time in a U.S. four-year degree program.

Degree Level:
- Undergraduate

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency is expected because the program is for U.S. undergraduate study.

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Last-dollar scholarship support toward the full cost of attendance not already covered by other financial aid and family contribution.
