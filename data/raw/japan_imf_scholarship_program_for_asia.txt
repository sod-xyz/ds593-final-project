Japan-IMF Scholarship Program for Asia (Japan)

Overview:
JISPA / Japan-IMF Scholarship Program for Asia. The Japan-IMF Scholarship Program for Asia is designed for junior government officials from key economic agencies in Asia and the Pacific. It provides opportunities to pursue graduate-level degrees in economics at leading Japanese universities and is connected to future leadership in economic policy.

Degree Level:
- Graduate / Master's

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers tuition, monthly stipend, travel, and related study expenses.
