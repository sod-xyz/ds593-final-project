Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships (Canada)

Overview:
Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships (Canada). The Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships program aims to attract and retain world-class doctoral students by supporting individuals who demonstrate leadership skills and a high standard of scholarly achievement in graduate studies. The scholarship is available to both Canadian and international students pursuing PhD degrees at participating Canadian institutions.

Degree Level:
- PhD

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: conditional
- Details: English or French depending on institution

Funding:
- Funding type: partial
- Details: Provides a fixed annual doctoral scholarship amount; may not cover all costs depending on institution and living expenses.
