AAUW International Fellowships (USA)

Overview:
AAUW International Fellowships. AAUW provides fellowship and grant opportunities that support women in education and career advancement. International Fellowships have historically supported women who are not U.S. citizens or permanent residents for graduate or postgraduate study in the United States, though applicants should verify the current cycle, eligibility, and award rules on the AAUW fellowships page.S. study, gender equity, and non-U.S. citizens seeking fellowship support.

Degree Level:
- Graduate / Postgraduate

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required

Funding:
- Funding type: partial
- Details: Provides fellowship funding for study or research; usually not a complete full-cost scholarship.
