Eiffel Excellence Scholarship Program (France)

Overview:
Eiffel Excellence Scholarship Program (France). The Eiffel Excellence Scholarship Program is a French government scholarship programme for top international students at master's and doctoral levels. It is designed to help French higher education institutions attract excellent foreign students in priority fields. Applications are usually submitted by French institutions rather than directly by students.

Degree Level:
- Master's / PhD

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: conditional
- Details: French or English depending on program

Funding:
- Funding type: partial
- Details: Usually provides a monthly allowance and some services; tuition is generally not covered by the Eiffel scholarship itself.
