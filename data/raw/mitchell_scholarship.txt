George J. Mitchell Scholarship (Ireland, U.S. citizens only)

The George J. Mitchell Scholarship supports future American leaders for one year of graduate study in Ireland or Northern Ireland. It is prestigious but restricted to U.S. citizens.

Mongolia Eligibility:
- Mongolian citizens are not eligible unless they are also U.S. citizens.

Eligibility:
- Applicants must be U.S. citizens.
- Applicants must be between 18 and 30 years old during the application year.
- Applicants must have a bachelor's degree before beginning the scholarship.

Degree Level:
- Master's / Graduate study

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency is required for graduate study in Ireland or Northern Ireland.

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers tuition, accommodation, stipend, and travel support.
