Swedish Institute Scholarships (Sweden)

Overview:
Swedish Institute Scholarships (Sweden). Swedish Institute scholarships support international students applying for eligible master's programmes in Sweden. The Swedish Institute Scholarships for Global Professionals are intended for applicants from selected eligible countries and usually emphasize leadership, professional experience, and contribution to sustainable development. Applicants normally need to apply separately for master's admission and for the scholarship.

Degree Level:
- Master's

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency required

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers tuition, monthly living allowance, insurance, and travel grant for eligible students.
