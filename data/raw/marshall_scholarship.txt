Marshall Scholarship (United Kingdom, U.S. citizens only)

The Marshall Scholarship finances young Americans of high ability to study for a graduate degree in the United Kingdom. It is a prestigious scholarship, but it is restricted to U.S. citizens.

Mongolia Eligibility:
- Mongolian citizens are not eligible unless they also hold U.S. citizenship and meet the U.S. degree requirements.

Eligibility:
- Applicants must be citizens of the United States.
- Applicants must hold a first undergraduate degree from an accredited four-year college or university in the United States.
- Applicants must meet the GPA and timing requirements for the application cycle.

Degree Level:
- Master's / Graduate study

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: English proficiency is required because study takes place at UK universities.

Funding:
- Funding type: fully_funded_with_stipend
- Details: Typically covers university fees, living costs, travel, and other approved expenses.
