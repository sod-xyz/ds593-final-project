Boren Awards (United States citizens only)

Boren Scholarships and Fellowships fund U.S. students to study languages and regions important to U.S. national security. The awards are designed for U.S. citizens and include a federal service requirement.

Mongolia Eligibility:
- Mongolian citizens are not eligible unless they are also U.S. citizens.

Eligibility:
- Applicants must be U.S. citizens at the time of the national application deadline.
- Applicants must be at least 18 years old and meet education-level requirements.
- Applicants must study an eligible language or region.

Degree Level:
- Undergraduate / Graduate fellowship depending on route

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: required
- Details: Language study is central to the award; applicants propose language-focused study.

Funding:
- Funding type: partial
- Details: Provides scholarship or fellowship funding for study abroad; amount depends on award type and duration.
