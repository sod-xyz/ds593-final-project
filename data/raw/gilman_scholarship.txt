Benjamin A. Gilman International Scholarship (United States students only)

The Gilman Scholarship supports American undergraduate students with high financial need to study or intern abroad. It is a U.S. Department of State program intended for U.S. students.

Mongolia Eligibility:
- Mongolian citizens are not eligible unless they are studying as eligible U.S. students and meet the citizenship and federal aid requirements.

Eligibility:
- Applicants must be American undergraduate students.
- Applicants generally need to receive a Federal Pell Grant.
- Applicants must study or intern abroad in an eligible program.

Degree Level:
- Undergraduate study abroad / Internship abroad

Language Requirement:
- Requirement type: conditional
- Details: Language study may be part of the program but is not always required.

Funding:
- Funding type: partial
- Details: Provides a scholarship award for study or internship abroad; does not normally cover all costs.
